About Me

I'm a Mechanical Engineering graduate from Ashesi University with a passion for solving community problems through innovative design and biotechnology. My work bridges mechanical engineering, synthetic biology, and microfluidics to create solutions that matter to struggling communities.

üîß Battery Thermal Image

CFD-Based Battery Thermal Management

ANSYS Fluent | Lithium-ion Optimization

Optimized thermal performance of lithium-ion battery packs through cell geometry and spacing design. Compared cylindrical (18650/21700/32650), prismatic, and pouch cell configurations using MATLAB FEA and ANSYS Fluent. Determined 21700 cells with 4 mm spacing and liquid cooling as optimal, achieving 302 K max temperature with uniform gradients.

Hydrogel Water Dynamics Modeling

Differential Equations | Agriculture

Developed differential equation models characterizing water absorption and release in superabsorbent hydrogels.
